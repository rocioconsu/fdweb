About me

Hi! My name is Rocío Consuegra and I'm a graphic design student currently studying at UDIT, in Madrid. I'm passionate about visual communication and love exploring different areas of design, especially UX/UI, typography, and editorial design.

My style is clean, colorful, and often inspired by everyday life and modern culture. I like mixing digital and traditional techniques to find creative solutions.
Design allows me to express ideas and connect with people in a visual way. I'm always curious to learn new tools, explore trends, and grow as a designer. Outside of design, I enjoy drawing, watching films, and discovering new music.

Thanks for visiting my portfolio — I hope you enjoy my work!
