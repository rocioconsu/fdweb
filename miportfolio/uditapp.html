UDIT App

A digital tool designed for the student community

This project is the design of a mobile application created for students at UDIT. Based on the university’s existing visual identity, the app maintains consistency with the current website but introduces new features that respond to students’ real needs.

The app includes key sections such as academic news, event calendars, extracurricular activities, digital resources, important notices, and quick access to university services, among others. The user experience has been carefully designed to be clear, intuitive, and efficient, focusing on easy navigation and quick access to relevant information.

The goal of this project is to offer a functional tool that not only improves internal communication, but also strengthens the sense of community among students — promoting a more connected, participatory, and organized university life.
