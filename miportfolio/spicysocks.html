Spicy Socks

Brand and web design for a fun and versatile identity

This project involved creating a sock brand and designing its website using Figma, with a fresh and playful concept aimed at a broad and diverse audience. Spicy Socks offers socks with unique prints — from fruits and animals to geometric patterns — with styles available for men, women, and children.

The website design reflects the bold and energetic identity of the brand. The background features illustrations of chili peppers, making a direct reference to both the brand name and its logo. There is also a limited edition collection of chili-patterned socks, reinforcing the visual and thematic identity of Spicy Socks.

The site’s structure is designed to offer a smooth and user-friendly experience, with clear product categories, engaging visuals, and a tone that connects with a target audience that appreciates originality, humor, and design. Also this porject involves creating a responsible design so this web page is available for mobile, tablet and computer screens.
