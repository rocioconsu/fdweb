Brota App

App for Urban Gardening

Brota is a mobile application designed to help users create and maintain urban gardens, encouraging a lifestyle that is more sustainable, natural, and self-sufficient—even in the heart of the city.

The app offers a full set of tools to guide users from the planning stage to the daily care of their garden. It includes video tutorials, community forums, personalized reminders, and helpful tips, making it useful for both beginners and more experienced gardeners.

The visual identity of Brota follows a minimalist and efficient design, with a clean interface focused on easy navigation. The color palette—green, white, and grey—reinforces the connection with nature and brings a sense of calm and simplicity.

More than just a tool, Brota promotes values like autonomy, community, and sustainability, helping people not only grow their own food but also a more mindful way of living.
