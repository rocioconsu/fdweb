Casa Tarradellas

A friendly, sustainable, and modern redesign

The redesign of Casa Tarradellas aims to modernize the brand and bring it closer to a broader, more sustainability-conscious audience. By shifting the visual approach, a softer, less aggressive color palette has been chosen, conveying warmth and trust, in contrast to the boldness of the previous designs.

In addition to the color update, the brand’s visual communication has been reimagined. The advertising becomes more inclusive and accessible, with a strong emphasis on handmade illustrations that give the brand an authentic, human touch. As for the packaging, recyclable materials are now prioritized, aligning with the brand's commitment to sustainability.

The goal of the redesign is to project a more contemporary image that reflects the brand’s core values while making it more approachable, reliable, and aligned with today’s environmental concerns. The aim is not only to update the aesthetics but also to improve the overall perception of the brand, making it a modern and responsible choice for consumers.
