Veranos de la Villa

A poster that celebrates the joy of summer in Madrid

This poster, designed for the cultural festival Veranos de la Villain Madrid, presents an image that blends the everyday with the festive, the real with the imagined. Using collage as the main technique, the piece combines a photograph of El Retiro Park — one of the city's most iconic green spaces — with warm and colorful illustrations that capture the spirit of summer.

In the foreground, three children, drawn in a simple and expressive style, are shown playing and dancing, bringing movement, freshness, and a sense of innocence to the composition. Colorful illustrated garlands are hung across the trees, adding a festive and joyful atmosphere. The title “Veranos de la Villa” is also illustrated, with a custom rounded type style that reinforces the poster’s friendly and inviting tone.

The color palette contrasts the soft, natural tones of the photograph — greens and beiges — with brighter hues used for the characters: orange, yellow, green, and blue. This visual contrast helps highlight the illustrated elements, emphasizing the connection between the real city and a playful summer fantasy.

The result is a poster that celebrates outdoor life, childhood, and creativity, reflecting the inclusive and cheerful spirit of this cultural event in Madrid.
