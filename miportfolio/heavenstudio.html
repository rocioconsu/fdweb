Heaven Studio

Shaping ideas, elevating design

Heaven Studio is a visual identity created for a graphic design studio whose main goal is to turn clients’ ideas into reality — and make them the best they can be. The concept is built around a creative vision that feels almost celestial: transforming thoughts, sketches, and ambitions into strong, unique, and well-crafted designs.

The logo features a stylized H combined with an angel wing, merging the brand’s core values into one symbol — imagination, care, and aspiration. This graphic element anchors the visual identity, supported by a universe of symbols like clouds, wings, and light that reinforce the studio’s kind, creative, and dream-like personality.

The color palette mixes soft pastel tones (pink, light blue, light green) with their deeper complements (burgundy, dark blue, dark green), creating a balanced contrast that brings both gentleness and strength. This combination allows the brand to adapt to different types of projects, from delicate to bold, while staying visually consistent.

Heaven Studio is more than a design service — it’s a collaborative experience where the client feels that their ideas truly “take flight.” A brand that communicates professionalism, warmth, and creativity through its visual identity.
